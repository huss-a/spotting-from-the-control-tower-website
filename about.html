​Spotting From The Control Tower started for one reason, telling the stories that matter. We focus our passion for highlighting positivity, providing learning opportunities and bridging the gap between pilots and controllers.

We hold ourselves accountable for being the difference and being the change, always aspiring to put quality over quantity and to make sure the members of the community are always heard.

Looking towards the future, our goals is to spread awareness, passion and sense of unity between the Infinite Flight users, aiming to improve the overall experience for everybody as a whole. ​
